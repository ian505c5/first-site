Project Overview

One of the concepts covered in User Experience was the use of task flow charts to help streamline a user's experience as they attempt to complete a process. By mapping out the steps the user must take to completion, you can eliminate redunancies and identify dead ends. We were tasked with identifying a multi-step task users undertake on a website, and creating a chart to document the process.

Overview

The task I chose to document was placing an order for delivery through GrubHub.com. GrubHub is a US web and mobile company that lets users find delivery and takeout restaurants near them and order online for free. Users can either use the service as a guest, or sign up for an account which provides them with additional features such as one-click ordering and order histories.


Overall the process was straightforward and efficient. Instructions were clear at every step, content was filtered appropriatly, and navigation between different stages was smooth. The only recomendations I could offer pertained to orders made by groups, rather than individuals. An option to split the final bill and tip evenly through multiple payments would be a great convenience for large parties, but its exclusion by no means detracts from the experience.
